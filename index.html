Superior Masonry Thunder Bay Ltd. Has been Thunder Bay and surrounding areas leading Masonry Contractor since 1974. We specialize in all types or masonry work ranging from Commercial, Industrial, Residential, Restoration and landscaping.

What we do

Residential

Superior Masonry Thunder Bay Ltd. Has the ability to work with and complete any type of fireplaces, stone or brick veneer products you may require in your residential design.

Commercial

Superior Masonry Thunder Bay Ltd. Is well versed in all types of commercial Masonry projects ranging from small to medium sized projects. These projects include but are not limited to schools, clinics, office buildings, warehouses, etc.

Restoration

Superior Masonry Thunder Bay Ltd. Is a certified masonry restoration specialist having completed major restoration work on various buildings and government buildings in Thunder Bay and surrounding area.

Industrial

Superior Masonry Thunder Bay Ltd. Has the capability to complete heavy industrial work including kiln, tank and boiler work.

Landscaping & External Works

Superior Masonry Thunder Bay Ltd. can complete landscape projects ranging from lock stone pavers, concrete sidewalks, stone walk-ways and basement water-proofing.

While continuing with residential construction, Ugo with the help of his son Joseph Gazzola evolved superior masonry from solely residential masonry services to a well-rounded company that now specializes in all different aspects of masonry work.
